Arriving to Medellín

By plane

José María Córdova (MDE) international airport

The airport is located in the region of Rionegro 29 km (18 miles) southeast of the center of the city of Medellin. Buses, minibuses and taxis connect the airport to different parts of the metropolitan area. The average journey to the city center is around 40 minutes. The approximate cost for the trip between the airport and the city center is $ 54,000 pesos (20USD) by Taxi and $12.000 (4 USD) by bus. More info: http://www.aeropuertojosemariacordova.com/en/

Enrique Olaya Herrera (EOH) airport

The airport is located in the metropolitan area of the city 7 km (4 miles) away of the center of the city of Medellin. Buses, minibuses and taxis connect the airport to different parts of the metropolitan area. The average journey to the city center is under 20 minutes. Taxi rides can be taken to most parts of the city for under $25000 (10USD) and the average cost of a bus is under $3000 (2 USD). More info: http://www.aeropuertoolayaherrera.gov.co/

By regional bus

There are two bus terminals within the metropolitan area, the south and north terminals. Buses, minibuses and taxis connect the terminals to different parts of the metropolitan area. The average journey from any of them to the city center is under 20 minutes. Taxi rides can be taken to most parts of the city for under $25000 (10USD) and the average cost of a bus is under $3000 (2 USD). More info: http://www.terminalesmedellin.com/

Practical information

Transportation within the city

Public transportation within the city is very convenient and reaches virtually every spot within the metropolitan area. The city counts with a metropolitan light-train (Metro), buses and taxis. A taxi ride between most parts of the city is under $25000 (10USD). The Metro system also has a few lines of air cable cars. One of them (Line L) is a tourist-oriented line which is connected to Arví Park. Telephone numbers of some taxi companies in the city are: Flota Bernal +57-4-4448882, Tax individual +57-4-4440888, Coopebombas +57-4-4440000 and Tax Super +57-4-4441111. Some smartphone apps as EasyTaxi and Uber also have drivers in the city.

Weather

Medellín is located in a valley surrounded by mountains. Its location close to the Equator and its altitude ( 1,500 m - 4,900 ft above sea level) combine to a warm tropical weather with occasional rains depending on the season. The average temperatures are between 17° C (62° F) and 28° C (83° F) all year long. More info: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medell%C3%ADn

Information for International Participants

Colombia requires either a visa or the payment of a reciprocity fee for certain countries, please consult if any of these apply to you.
We recommend participants coming from outside of Colombia to have emergency medical coverage while in Colombia. If your own health insurance does not cover you while in Colombia such an insurance can be obtained for reasonable prices in any travel agency or directly from insurance companies like AIG-Travel Guard or Allianz.
